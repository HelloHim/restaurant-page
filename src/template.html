Welcome to GlassRoots

At GlassRoots, we believe great food starts with strong foundations. Housed in a light-filled space, our kitchen turns out dishes built from honest, seasonal ingredients and a healthy respect for where they came from. Expect a menu that changes with the seasons, a wine list chosen to match, and a room designed to feel like home. Come hungry, stay a while, and taste what happens when food is grown with care.
